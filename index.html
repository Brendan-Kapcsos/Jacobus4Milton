JAN JACOBUS HAS STAYED TRUE TO HIS WORD TO MILTON HOMEOWNERS –

and Jan has a plan to keep Milton on top!

Deerfield

Continue following through with the Deerfield Implementation Plan, a citizen driven project that both enhances the area economically by attracting prominent businesses and beautifies it for generations to come.

Growth and development

Respect Milton’s character and put homeowners’ wishes first. Work to develop larger lots and save farms. No new sewer. Period.

Public safety

Preserve Milton’s reputation as one of Georgia’s safest cities and put first responders first, offering support and compensation that keeps them in Milton.

Parks and greenspace

Protect our natural heritage, break ground on the new Deerfield Active Park while maintaining and growing our passive parks.

ABOUT JAN

Jan has lived in Milton since before the City’s incorporation, moving to the Crooked Creek subdivision in the area that would become Milton in 1997.

Jan has served Milton on City Council since 2022 - prior to serving on Council, Jan sat on the Milton Planning Commission. Professionally, Jan worked for over three decades and served in various leadership positions for Abbott Laboratories, an international healthcare company. He holds a double major degree in both marketing and economics from Ashland University.

Jan is a proud father of three and grandfather of seven.
